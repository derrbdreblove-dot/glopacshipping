Prohibited & Restricted Items

To comply with international regulations and ensure safety, certain items are prohibited or restricted from shipment via Glopac Shipping Express.

Important: Shipping prohibited items may result in seizure, penalties, or permanent account suspension.

Prohibited Items (Not Accepted)

  • Illegal drugs, narcotics, and controlled substances
  • Weapons, firearms, ammunition, explosives
  • Hazardous materials (flammables, corrosives, radioactive items)
  • Counterfeit goods and pirated media
  • Live animals or animal remains
  • Pornographic or obscene materials
  • Human remains or body parts

Restricted Items (Special Approval Required)

  • Luxury goods and high-value jewelry
  • Medical supplies and pharmaceuticals
  • Electronics with lithium batteries
  • Artwork and antiques
  • Perishable goods

Customer Responsibility

Customers are responsible for ensuring that shipped items comply with destination country laws and customs regulations.
