About Glopac Shipping Express

Who We Are

Glopac Shipping Express is an international logistics and freight service provider specializing in secure, trackable, and efficient global shipping solutions. We support businesses and individuals with reliable transportation, customs handling, and real-time shipment visibility.

What We Do

  • International parcel and freight shipping
  • Real-time tracking and shipment history
  • Customs clearance and duty/tax coordination
  • Secure handling of high-value goods
  • Customer and admin communication via shipment chat

Our Mission

To simplify global logistics by combining transparency, technology, and operational excellence—ensuring every shipment reaches its destination safely and efficiently.

Regions We Serve

Glopac Shipping Express operates across: North America, South America, Europe, and Asia.
