Policies

These policies explain how Glopac Shipping Express handles shipping, delivery, customs holds, and payments. You can adjust wording anytime.

Important: If a shipment is held for duties/taxes, only the account owner can view the fee amount and submit payment.

1) Tracking & Status Updates

Tracking events are provided as shipment scans occur. Times may vary based on local hubs, customs, and partner carriers.

  • “Label Created” means shipping info was created but the parcel may not yet be received.
  • “In Transit” means the parcel is moving through hubs.
  • “On Hold” may occur during customs review, missing documents, or payment requirements.
  • “Delivered” means shipment reached destination facility and was completed.

2) Customs, Duties & Taxes

Some shipments require duties or taxes before release. This depends on destination rules and declared value. When fees are required, the shipment status will show “On Hold”.

  • Fees are shown only to the shipment owner account.
  • After payment submission, status may show “Pending Verification”.
  • Once verified by admin, the shipment is released and returns to “In Transit”.

3) Prohibited & Restricted Items

Customers must comply with local and international shipping laws. Some items are prohibited or restricted.

  • Hazardous materials, illegal substances, and prohibited weapons are not accepted.
  • Perishable items may require special packaging/approval.
  • High-value items may require documentation and insurance options.

4) Claims & Liability

If a shipment is damaged, missing, or delayed unusually, you may file a claim through the Claims page.
